## What I Do

- **Check-up & Diagnosis:** complete analysis of deck equipment to verify its condition and efficiency
- **Deck Layout Optimization:** customized study of the deck plan to make handling the boat smoother and more intuitive
- **Hardware Overhaul:** professional servicing of winches, blocks and every deck component
- **Standing Rigging:** thorough inspection of mast and rigging. As an operator certified for work at height, I carry out inspections and interventions directly at the masthead
- **Running Rigging:** assessment and replacement of halyards and sheets with latest-generation materials
- **Professional Splicing:** handcrafted splices on every type of line
- **Composite Repairs:** when needed, targeted interventions on resins and composite materials
